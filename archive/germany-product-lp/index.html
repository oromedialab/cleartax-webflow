The Strategic Question

Why ViDA 2030 changes the platform decision

The 2027 mandate is a format change — XRechnung instead of PDF. Any reasonable e-invoicing tool can handle it. If 2027 were the only deadline, you could buy a point solution next quarter and move on.

ViDA 2030 is a data-flow change. Every cross-border B2B invoice issued or received in the EU triggers a real-time report to your tax authority — within five days, in structured form, with line-level detail. A 2027-only tool can't do this. It wasn't designed for the wire.

The CFOs and Heads of Tax who get this right pick their platform once, in 2026, and don't touch it again until 2030+. The ones who get it wrong are budgeting for a second platform migration before this one is even live.
