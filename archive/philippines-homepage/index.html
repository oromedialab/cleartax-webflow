The mandate explained

The BIR e-invoicing mandate is here.
It's bigger than your IT team thinks.

Revenue Regulation 11-2025 is not a form update. It's a fundamental change to how Philippine businesses issue, sign, and report invoices and the deadline is December 31, 2026.

What you must comply with

Transmission model

Post-audit. Invoices delivered directly to buyers, no BIR clearance required.

Applicability

Large Taxpayers, e-commerce businesses, and any enterprise using ERP or accounting software. In scope regardless of sales volume.

Acceptable formats

JSONs only. PDFs and scanned invoices do not qualify. Digital signature required on every invoice.

Transactions covered

Sales invoices, official receipts, service billings, credit and debit notes. If any branch is in scope, all locations must comply.

Penalties & incentives

50–100% tax deduction on implementation costs for early adopters. Non-compliance: invoices invalidated, input tax deductions lost.

Step 01
CAS Registration Support
Before you can issue a single invoice, BIR requires an Acknowledgement Certificate. An incomplete submission restarts the clock. Our compliance consultants walk your team through the Compliance Dossier Report submission to your RDO, so nothing gets rejected.
Step 02
Structured Invoice Issuance
ClearTax pulls data from your ERP, transforms it into BIR-compliant JSON, applies the required JWS digital signature (RS256), and delivers the signed invoice directly to your buyer. No BIR clearance gate. No manual steps.
Step 03
EIS Onboarding & PTT Support
Your Permit to Transmit and EIS Certificate are issued to you, not your vendor. We manage the joint UAT with BIR and ensure your team is certified on time, so the 3-day clock never catches you unprepared.
Step 04
Automated ESRS Reporting
Once BIR infrastructure goes live, ClearTax automatically transmits the same invoice payload to BIR EIS within the 3-day window. Idempotent retries, cancellation and amendment payloads are handled by the platform, not your team.
Step 05
Reconciliation & Audit Readiness
ClearTax continuously reconciles your GL data against EIS-reported invoice data. AI surfaces mismatches, value gaps, and missing documents before BIR flags them, so audits stay routine reviews rather than board-level escalations.

What is e‑invoicing in the Philippines?
E‑invoicing in the Philippines is the BIR‑mandated process of issuing invoices in structured JSON format and reporting them to the Electronic Invoicing System (EIS) at eis.bir.gov.ph. It is mandated to take effect from 31 December 2026 under RR No. 26‑2025 for large taxpayers, e‑commerce platforms, and ERP users.

The mandate requires covered taxpayers to issue invoices and receipts as signed JSON files and transmit them to the BIR within three days. ClearTax offers a complete solution that removes the need for in‑house development by finance and IT teams.
