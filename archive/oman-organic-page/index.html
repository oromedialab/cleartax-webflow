Key Requirements

Oman E-Invoicing Mandate at a Glance

Everything your business needs to know about the Fawtara framework before the mandate goes live.

Transmission Model

Decentralised 5-corner model (Fawtara). Invoices flow through OTA-accredited service providers ensuring end-to-end traceability.

Applicability

All VAT-registered businesses in Oman. VAT group members must each follow e-invoicing procedures and share a single service provider.

Acceptable Formats

Invoices must be issued in XML or PDF/A-3 format. A digital certificate is required to ensure reliability and verification.

Transmission Methods

All invoices must be transmitted via an OTA-accredited Service Provider such as ClearTax.

Archival

Invoices must be archived for 10 years — 5 years in the system and 5 years in an electronic archive.

Penalties

Penalties applied per regulations for non-compliance. A grace period is available before full enforcement begins.
