What's the case for a native SAP add-on?

If your goal is to move from middleware-and-spreadsheet workarounds to a fully embedded e-invoicing posture inside SAP, ClearTax is built for that. We don't sit outside SAP poking at it through batch jobs — we deploy a certified ABAP add-on inside your SAP system, shipped through STMS like any other transport.

This means data extraction happens via standard SAP objects (VBRK, BSEG, BKPF, ACDOCA), validation runs inside your SAP environment, and your tax team works from a Fiori-style cockpit in the same client they already use.

The result: every invoice your SAP system creates becomes a compliant, structured e-invoice — in the format your jurisdiction demands — without leaving SAP.

How it works

A compliant e-invoice, end to end in SAP

Six capabilities, one transport. Here's how data moves from billing document to tax authority — and what ClearTax does at every step.

1. Source

SAP ECC / S/4HANA

  • SD billing (VBRK)
  • FI postings (BSEG / ACDOCA)
  • MM credit / debit notes
  • Output determination NACE
2. Extract & transform

ClearTax ABAP Add-on

  • Real-time via OData / RFC
  • Batch via IDoc INVOIC02
  • Field mapping cockpit (Fiori)
  • BAdI-extensible for Z-fields
3. Validate & submit

Tax authority

  • Format conversion (UBL/CII/Factur-X)
  • Schema & business validation
  • Submission to PA / PPF / KSeF
  • Status sync back to SAP

SAP-native ingestion

Pre-built RICEF objects deployed via STMS. ClearTax reads from VBRK, BSEG, BKPF, ACDOCA and KNA1/LFA1 — no flat-file exports, no third-party middleware. BAdI hooks for output determination ensure invoices route through compliance before printing.

IDoc INVOIC02 OData v2/v4 BAPI_INCOMINGINVOICE BTE 00503301

Format engine

ClearTax converts SAP output into UBL 2.1, CII, Factur-X (France); FA(2) XML (KSeF Poland); ZATCA Phase-2 XML (Saudi); FTA UBL (UAE); Peppol BIS 3.0 (Belgium). One source object, every output format.

UBL 2.1 CII Factur-X Peppol BIS FA(2)

Real-time status sync

Every invoice carries lifecycle state back into SAP. The 14-status DGFiP lifecycle, KSeF UPO references, ZATCA QR codes, FTA acknowledgement IDs — all written back to Z-tables linked to your billing documents.

Fiori-style cockpit

A custom Fiori application gives tax and AR teams a single pane: failed validations, pending submissions, status by company code, by VAT registration, by mandate. Mass resubmission, exception management, and audit trails — inside SAP launchpad with PFCG authorisations.

FAQ

SAP customers, straight answers

The questions we get asked most by SAP basis teams, FI leads and CIOs scoping their e-invoicing project.

How does ClearTax actually deploy into our SAP system?
ClearTax ships as a native ABAP add-on in the /CLEARTAX/* namespace. Deployment: (1) SAINT/SPAM import into dev client, (2) SPRO configuration — company codes, mandate selection, RFC connection, (3) field mapping in Fiori cockpit, (4) transport through STMS to QA → Prod. No core SAP modifications. SAP support unaffected.
Which SAP versions are supported?
SAP ECC 6.0 from EHP7+, SAP S/4HANA on-premise 1709–2023, S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition, S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition via BTP connector, SAP Business One and ByDesign via API connectors.
How does this compare to SAP Document Compliance (DRC)?
ClearTax is the alternative when you need mandates DRC doesn't cover, want a single platform for all geographies, need confirmed PA accreditation in France, want faster go-live, or want one vendor handling both submission and the full e-reporting cycle. Many customers run DRC for one or two countries and ClearTax for everything else.
What does a typical 4–6 week deployment look like?
Week 1: scope & design workshop. Week 2: transport to DEV, RFC config, first test invoices. Week 3: transport to QA, UAT with real data. Week 4: parallel run in production mirror mode. Weeks 5–6: go-live and hypercare. Multi-entity or multi-country environments typically take 10 weeks.
What happens when the mandate changes?
Mandate changes are handled in the ClearTax cloud, not your SAP system. When DGFiP publishes new specs or ZATCA changes schemas, ClearTax updates the format engine. Your add-on continues sending the same payload. Your SAP team rarely needs to ship a new transport.
How is data residency and security handled?
ISO 27001 and SOC 2 Type II certified. EU data stays in EU AWS regions; UAE in AWS Middle East; KSA in-Kingdom. TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 at rest. SAP-side auth via RFC destinations with rotating tokens, optionally mTLS. Fiori cockpit access controlled by SAP PFCG roles.
