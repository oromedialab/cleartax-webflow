▸ The regulatory horizon

From September 2026 to ViDA, in three moves.

The mandate doesn't end at French go-live. Here's what's coming and when.

France · Go-live

2028 EU-wide
ViDA · Phase 1
Structured e-invoicing mandatory for all intra-EU B2B transactions.
2030 EU-wide

▸ The question most teams aren't asking

Most teams are solving for today. Very few are ready for what follows.

The conversations happening inside most organisations right now are focused on one question: will we be live before September 2026? That question is the right one. It is also incomplete.

▸ The architecture question
The ERP configurations, data architectures, and platform decisions being finalised right now for France will either lay the groundwork for ViDA readiness — or create a second, more complex remediation project in two years.
What's at stake
  • Build twice, not once France-only architectures need rework in 2028 — the cost of a second remediation programme inside two years.
  • ERP capability gaps compound Gaps that appear first in France implementation compound into systemic failures under ViDA's real-time cross-checks.
  • Mandate-specific lock-in Format-specific choices made today can lock you out of a mandate-agnostic compliance architecture tomorrow.
