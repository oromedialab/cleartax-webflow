Understanding the global shift

Governments are accelerating e-invoicing mandates. Here's what it means for your business.

Closing the VAT gap
Governments lose trillions annually to VAT/GST fraud. Real-time e-invoicing is the primary tool to recover this revenue and cut evasion.
Automated compliance
Structured formats like XML, UBL, and Peppol let machines validate and submit invoices automatically, reducing errors and audit risk.
Faster payments
E-invoicing accelerates order-to-cash cycles. Automated delivery and matching leads to faster approvals and shorter payment terms.
Global interoperability
Peppol bridges national systems for cross-border e-invoicing. The EU's ViDA initiative will make intra-EU digital reporting mandatory by 2030.
