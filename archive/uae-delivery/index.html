The 16 use cases

16 invoice scenarios. One platform.

The standard tax invoice is the baseline — around 50 mandatory fields. The other fifteen use cases are each that baseline plus a small set of fields that switch on for that transaction, for a specific regulatory reason. We've already mapped every one of those deltas — so your team doesn't have to.

The baseline

A standard tax invoice: ~50 mandatory fields, structured UBL-XML, and a Peppol ID built as 0235 + 10-digit TIN . Everything else is measured against this.

The fifteen deltas

Each variation adds or removes specific fields — a beneficiary here, an invoicing period there, a principal's TRN, a delivery block. Get the condition wrong and the field is missing, or present when forbidden. Either way: rejected.
