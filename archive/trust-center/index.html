Certifications

Third-party verified. No self-assessments.

Every certification was issued by an independent auditing body after evidence review. These are not checkboxes — they are the standards your procurement and legal teams will ask for.

🛡️
Global
ISO 27001:2022
The current international standard for information security management. Independent auditors verify that controls across people, processes, and technology are actually working — not just documented.
🔍
Global
SOC 2 Type II
Tests how well security, availability, and confidentiality controls operate over time. It proves controls are consistently followed day-to-day — not just designed on paper.
⚡
UAE
ISO 22301:2019
Defines how we prepare for, respond to, and recover from disruptions — cyber incidents, outages, or supply chain failures. Ensures tested business continuity plans and fast recovery for critical services.
🔒
EU & UAE
GDPR
EU data protection law setting strict rules on how personal data is collected, processed, stored, and shared. Emphasises privacy by design, user consent, transparency, and strong data subject rights.
📋
UAE
FTA Compliant
The UAE Federal Tax Authority's regulatory requirements for e-invoicing, VAT reporting, and tax data handling. A baseline requirement for any business registered for VAT in the UAE using our platform.
🏦
Saudi Arabia
SAMA
Saudi Arabian Monetary Authority cybersecurity framework for financial institutions and technology service providers. Covers governance, risk identification, protective controls, and incident response.
🇲🇾
Malaysia
RMiT Compliant
Bank Negara Malaysia's mandatory Risk Management in Technology framework. Covers cloud adoption, cyber resilience, IT service management, and third-party risk for regulated financial institutions.
📜
India
GSP / ASP / IRP
Certified GST Suvidha Provider, Application Service Provider, and Invoice Registration Portal authorised by GSTN/NIC. Aligned with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023.
