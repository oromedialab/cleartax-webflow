France E-Invoicing Readiness Assessment

Evaluate how prepared your finance function is for real-time, global compliance, from regulatory complexity to operational scale.

Take the ~8 minute survey to unlock early access to the full report.
⚖️
Section 1 of 5
Regulatory Awareness
Q1
Have you mapped both e-invoicing (domestic B2B) and e-reporting (B2C and cross-border) as separate obligations across all your French entities?
Q2
Have you selected and contracted a Plateforme Agréée (PA)?
⚡ Your answer will reveal the next relevant follow-up question below.
Q2A
How compatible is your contracted PA with your ERP outputs and existing EDI flows?
Q2B
If you have not yet selected a PA, how thoroughly have you assessed what you need from one?
Q3
Have all your French entities registered in the DGFiP central directory with correct SIREN/SIRET and routing addresses?
🖥️
Section 2 of 5
Technical Infrastructure
Q4
Can your ERP generate all required French e-invoice formats (Factur-X, UBL 2.1, CII) with mandatory fields, lifecycle status codes, and payment data — automatically?
Q5
Are you technically ready to extract and submit e-reporting data (B2C summaries, cross-border transactions, payment data for services) to the DGFiP on schedule?
Q6
Are your AR and AP workflows designed to act on invoice lifecycle statuses (submitted, received, rejected, paid) in real time?
⚙️
Section 3 of 5
Process Maturity
Q7
Have you defined and tested compliant workflows for complex transaction types — credit notes, intercompany invoices, self-billing, and transactions with non-French counterparts?
Q8
Have you reviewed the accuracy of the invoice data that will become visible to the DGFiP in real time — and put pre-submission controls in place?
Q9
Have you formally assessed your penalty exposure (up to €15 per invoice, €15,000/year cap) and put documented mitigations in place?
🏢
Section 4 of 5
Organisational Readiness
Q10
Has a fully scoped budget been approved — covering PA fees, ERP development, format testing, e-reporting infrastructure, internal resource time, and post-go-live support?
Q11
How confident are you that your PA and ERP vendor will keep pace with France's evolving mandate specifications?
🔭
Section 5 of 5
Future Readiness
Q12
Is your French e-invoicing architecture designed to scale into a pan-European compliance platform as EU ViDA takes effect by 2030?
Q13
Has your organisation quantified and planned to capture the operational gains from the mandate — touchless AP/AR, automated posting, and faster month-end close?
Response Scale: L1 = Early Stage L2 = Developing L3 = Progressing L4 = Advanced
