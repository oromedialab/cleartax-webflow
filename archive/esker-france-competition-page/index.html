The France Mandate

The Reality of France's E-Invoicing Mandate

This is not a soft deadline. The DGFiP has locked the timeline — and there are no extensions expected.

September 2026
Large Enterprises
Must receive and issue structured e-invoices via PPF or an accredited PDP. PDF invoices become non-compliant from this date.
September 2027
Mid-Market & SMEs
All remaining B2B businesses must be fully onboarded. No grace period for late adoption.
