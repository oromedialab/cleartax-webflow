14 · The Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

The questions Philippine finance and tax leaders ask us most often.

Is my company in scope for the Philippine e-invoicing mandate? +
If you're classified as a Large Taxpayer, run an e-commerce business, or use ERP or accounting software — you're in scope under RR 11-2025. This applies regardless of revenue size, and if any branch is in scope, all your locations must comply.
What's the deadline? +
Wave 1 is December 31, 2026. Large Taxpayers, e-commerce businesses, and ERP users must issue compliant e-invoices by this date. Wave 2 (exporters, incentive-based businesses) and ESRS live reporting follow under a separate regulation.
What format does BIR require? +
Structured JSON with a JWS digital signature (RS256). PDFs are explicitly excluded under RR 11-2025. Plain email invoices are also invalid.
What happens if we miss the deadline? +
Non-compliant invoices are invalidated. You lose the right to claim input tax deductions on those transactions. BIR publishes lists of non-compliant companies publicly. And your Acknowledgement Certificate can be revoked on audit.
Can ClearTax work with our existing ERP? +
Yes. ClearTax integrates with SAP (R3, HANA, B1, ECC), Oracle (EBS, Fusion, NetSuite, JD Edwards), Microsoft Dynamics (NAV, AX, 365), Shopify, and 95+ custom ERPs. Our AI-powered connector maps your data automatically — setup takes days, not months.
Will we need a new integration project every time BIR changes something? +
No. Regulatory updates — new fields, amended schemas, format changes — are deployed on the ClearTax platform. Your ERP connector stays unchanged after go-live.
What's the difference between Track A and Track B? +
Track A governs invoice issuance: generating a structured JSON, signing it with JWS/RS256, and delivering it to the buyer from BIR-registered software. Track B governs reporting: transmitting that same payload to BIR's Electronic Invoicing System within 3 calendar days. You need both to be compliant.
What is the Acknowledgement Certificate (AC), and how do we get it? +
The Acknowledgement Certificate is BIR's confirmation that your invoicing software is registered and compliant. It's a prerequisite before you can issue a single valid e-invoice. ClearTax's compliance consultants guide your Compliance Dossier Report submission to your RDO — incomplete submissions restart the clock.
Are there incentives for early adoption? +
Yes. Under RR 11-2025, early adopters are entitled to 50–100% tax deductions on implementation costs. The earlier you go live, the more of your investment becomes deductible.
Can ClearTax handle high-volume retail and e-commerce scenarios? +
Yes. The ClearTax platform processes over 1 billion e-invoices annually across India, Saudi Arabia, the UAE, Malaysia, and other markets — with a capacity of 10,000 e-invoices per second and 200–300ms generation latency. Spike loads, flash sales, and fiscal year-end peaks are handled without degradation.
