## Design Context

### Users
B2B finance decision-makers (CFOs, Tax Heads, Finance Directors) working at mid-to-large UAE enterprises preparing for the 2026–27 e-invoicing mandate. They arrive via LinkedIn, email invites, or Cleartax CRM touchpoints — usually on desktop during work hours, sometimes mobile in evenings. Jobs-to-be-done differ by page type: event/roundtable pages = evaluate whether to attend and RSVP; product/mandate pages = understand deadlines, assess vendors, justify internal buy-in.

### Brand Personality
Trustworthy, compliance-grade, urgent-but-composed. Three words: **precise · authoritative · current**. Cleartax sells to regulated enterprises — the voice has to earn senior-finance trust on the first scroll. No playful toy-like gestures. No salesy hype. Earn the RSVP like a KPMG invite, not a webinar landing page.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Baseline**: editorial-corporate. Crisp typography, asymmetric layouts, generous whitespace, strict card-based grids with 20-24px rounded corners and 32px+ gaps.
- **Brand palette**: Primary Deep Corporate Purple `#301B5E`, Neutral White `#FFFFFF`, Neutral Black `#000000`. Event/roundtable pages may swap in gold `#C9A961` as the prestige accent.
- **Secondary palette & Tags**: Pastel Lavender `#EDEBFA`, Pastel Peach `#FDF2ED`, Pastel Orange `#F6E0C5`. Semantic tags in Purple `#9879AF`, Orange `#E5AC82`, Cyan `#13839D`, Forest `#338878`.
- **Typography**: 
  - **H1 (Hero Heading)**: Nohemi Medium, 72px, left-aligned.
  - **H2 (Section Heading)**: Nohemi Medium, 48px, center-aligned.
  - **H3 (Card Heading)**: Nohemi Medium, 36px, left-aligned, Deep Corporate Purple `#301B5E`.
  - **Body**: Gilroy Regular, 24px, approx 1.5x line height.
- **Buttons**: Primary "Pill" CTA is solid Deep Corporate Purple `#301B5E`, fully rounded (50px border-radius) with white text and a top-right arrow (↗).
- **Hero Imagery**: 5-layer composition. Regional Skyline (airy background), Corporate Visionary, Holographic Tech Element (glowing 3D shape), Glassmorphism Trust Cards, and Functional Gradients fading to `#1A0B3B` at bottom.
- **Illustrations**: Custom 3D isometric vector art using Tech Blue (dominant), Soft Lilac, Mint Green (accent/success), and Pale Blue/Grey.
- **Badges**: Official native logos placed inside standardized white container cards.

### Design Principles
1. **Trust over flourish.** Every decorative element has to survive the question "would KPMG ship this?" Cut it if not.
2. **Asymmetrical Card System.** Avoid rigid grids. Mix horizontal rectangles and squares with generous 32px+ spacing to let complex data breathe.
3. **One accent per screen.** Gold or purple, not both. Accents work because they are rare. Mint Green `#ECF7ED` strictly reserved for success/verification.
4. **Cinematic Open-World Heroes.** Heroes are the most critical visual element. Never use standard office stock photos. Use the 5-layer open-world + holographic tech formula.
5. **Content-first typography.** Nohemi for structural headings, Gilroy for readable body text (1.5x line height). No gradient text, no uppercase paragraphs.
